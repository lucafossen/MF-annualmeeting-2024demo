We are researchers from MediaFutures, and are trying out a new method of recommending related articles for journalists and editors. You'll be using this survey to provide feedback on the quality of these recommendations

Here's how to complete the survey:

  1. Click any article listed on this page. A new page will open along with related article recommendations.

  2. On that page, rate how relevant each recommended article is compared to the original article. Rate between 1 to 10, with 1 being Totally irrelevant and 10 being Totally relevant

  3. Once you have at least five ratings, a “Proceed to questionnaire” button will appear. Click it to complete the usability survey.
